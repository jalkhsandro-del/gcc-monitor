# GCC Monitor — Product Requirements Document

## 1. Overview

GCC Monitor is a personal intelligence dashboard for a McKinsey consultant based in Dubai. It aggregates macro-economic data, curated news, deal flow, and regulatory signals across the GCC (UAE, KSA, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman) and Egypt into a single daily-use interface.

**Target user**: Single user (the consultant). No auth, no multi-tenancy. Optimize for speed and information density.

**Core value proposition**: Walk into every client meeting knowing more about the macro context, industry news, and strategic signals than anyone else in the room.

---

## 2. Pages & modules

### 2.1 Home — Morning brief (`/`)

The landing page. A single-screen summary of the most important signals from the last 24 hours.

**Components:**
- **Top bar**: Current date (Gulf Standard Time), last data refresh timestamp
- **Market snapshot**: Horizontal strip showing Brent crude price + change, USD/AED, USD/SAR, gold, S&P 500 — all with sparkline micro-charts (7-day)
- **AI morning brief**: 3-4 paragraph AI-generated summary of the day's top stories, grouped by relevance to the user's four sectors (CPG, family businesses, private capital, tech). Generated by Claude at the 6 AM GST cron run.
- **Top stories**: 5-7 headline cards with source, timestamp, sector tag, country tag, and 2-line AI summary
- **Signals strip**: Compact row of alert badges — regulatory changes, leadership moves, notable deals — anything flagged as high-signal in the last 48 hours

**Behavior:**
- Entirely server-rendered. No client-side data fetching.
- Refreshes on page load (ISR with 15-minute revalidation).
- The AI brief is pre-generated and stored in the DB. If generation fails, show the top 5 article summaries as fallback.

### 2.2 Macro dashboard (`/macro`)

Financial and economic data for each GCC country + Egypt, presented in a tabbed interface.

**Tabs**: All GCC | UAE | KSA | Qatar | Kuwait | Bahrain | Oman | Egypt

**"All GCC" tab (default):**
- **Market indices table**: All 8 exchanges in a sortable table — index name, current value, daily change (%), YTD change (%), with color coding
- **Oil & energy strip**: Brent, WTI, Henry Hub natural gas, OPEC basket — current price, daily change, 30-day sparkline
- **Currency strip**: USD/AED, USD/SAR, USD/QAR, USD/KWD, USD/BHD, USD/OMR, USD/EGP — with daily change
- **Commodity strip** (CPG-relevant): Wheat, sugar, palm oil, dairy index — current price, daily change

**Country-specific tabs:**
- **Stock index chart**: Interactive line chart (30d, 90d, 1Y, YTD toggle)
- **Key stats card**: GDP growth (latest), CPI/inflation (latest), PMI (latest), unemployment, population, credit rating
- **Top movers**: Top 5 gainers and losers on that country's exchange, by daily % change
- **Sector breakdown**: Heatmap or treemap of exchange sectors by daily performance
- **Recent economic releases**: Table of recent data releases (PMI, CPI, trade balance, etc.) with date and value

**Data sources:**
- Yahoo Finance (`yahoo-finance2` package) for stock indices and commodity prices
- ExchangeRate-API or similar for FX rates
- Manual/cached data for macro stats (GDP, CPI, PMI) — updated monthly from IMF, central bank reports
- See @docs/data-sources.md for symbol mappings

**Update frequency:**
- Market data: every 15 minutes during trading hours via Vercel Cron
- Macro stats: manually updated monthly or via cron scraping central bank RSS

### 2.3 News feed (`/news`)

Curated, AI-classified news from 20+ GCC business sources, filterable by sector and country.

**Layout:**
- **Tab bar (sectors)**: All | CPG & Retail | Family Business & Conglomerates | Private Capital & SWFs | Tech & Digital | Energy | Real Estate & Construction | Regulation & Policy
- **Secondary filter**: Country dropdown (All, UAE, KSA, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Egypt)
- **Sort**: Latest first (default), Most relevant (AI-scored)
- **Article cards**: Source logo/name, headline (linked to original), publication time (relative), country badge, sector badge(s), 2-3 line AI summary, "Read more" link to original article

**Article ingestion pipeline:**
1. Vercel Cron triggers every 30 minutes
2. Fetch all RSS feeds in parallel (batched to respect rate limits)
3. Parse each article: title, link, description, pubDate, source
4. Dedup by URL (skip if already in DB)
5. Classify each new article:
   - Country: extract from source, keywords, or entity recognition
   - Sector(s): classify into one or more sectors using keyword matching + Claude classification for ambiguous cases
   - Signal score (1-5): how relevant/important is this to the user's consulting practice
6. Generate 2-3 sentence AI summary using Claude (batch, not per-article to save tokens)
7. Store in Postgres with all metadata

**Classification approach (start simple, iterate):**
- Phase 1: Keyword-based classification using dictionaries per sector/country
- Phase 2: Add Claude-based classification for articles that don't match keywords confidently
- Phase 3: Fine-tune with user feedback (thumbs up/down on classification)

**News sources (priority order — see @docs/data-sources.md for full list and RSS URLs):**

Tier 1 (daily essentials):
- AGBI (Arabian Gulf Business Insight) — premium GCC business analysis
- Arabian Business — broad GCC business news
- Zawya — financial news, M&A, market data
- Gulf Business — UAE-focused business news
- Arab News — Saudi-focused news
- The National (UAE) — quality UAE reporting

Tier 2 (sector-specific depth):
- Argaam — Saudi financial markets (English)
- TradeArabia — Bahrain-based, broad GCC trade
- Gulf News Business — UAE daily business
- Bloomberg Middle East — via RSS/Google News
- Reuters Middle East — via RSS
- Wamda — MENA tech/startup ecosystem
- Magnitt — MENA startup data (if API available)

Tier 3 (supplementary):
- Construction Week Online — GCC construction
- Hotelier Middle East — hospitality
- Al-Monitor — Middle East policy/geopolitics
- Middle East Eye — geopolitical analysis
- TechCrunch MENA tag — tech deals

### 2.4 Deal flow tracker (`/deals`)

Track M&A, IPOs, PE/VC investments, and SWF deployments across the GCC.

**Layout:**
- **Filter bar**: Deal type (All, M&A, IPO, PE/VC, SWF), Country, Sector, Date range
- **Deal cards**: Company/target name, deal type badge, value (if disclosed), acquirer/investor, date, 2-line summary, source link
- **Stats strip**: Deals this month, total value disclosed, top sectors, most active investors

**Data source:**
- Parsed from Zawya M&A feed, Arabian Business, AGBI
- AI-classified from general news feed (articles mentioning acquisition, investment, IPO, funding round, etc.)
- Manual curation option: the user can bookmark articles from the news feed as "deals"

**SWF-specific tracking:**
- Dedicated sub-filter for: PIF, ADIA, Mubadala, ADQ, QIA, KIA, OIA, EGX sovereign entities
- Track: target company, sector, deal size, co-investors, geography (domestic vs international)

### 2.5 Regulatory radar (`/regulatory`)

Track new laws, regulations, tax changes, labor reforms, and policy shifts across the GCC.

**Layout:**
- **Timeline view**: Chronological list of regulatory events, most recent first
- **Country tabs**: All | UAE | KSA | Qatar | Kuwait | Bahrain | Oman | Egypt
- **Category tags**: Tax, Labor, Corporate governance, FDI, Data protection, Sector-specific
- **Event cards**: Title, country, category, effective date (if known), impact summary (AI-generated), source link

**Data source:**
- Filtered from the main news feed (articles classified as "regulation" or "policy")
- Supplemented by government gazette RSS where available (UAE MOE, Saudi CMA, etc.)

### 2.6 Vision tracker (`/vision`)

Track progress on GCC national transformation programs and megaprojects.

**Layout:**
- **Country cards**: One card per country showing the vision name, key targets, and a progress indicator
  - Saudi Vision 2030
  - We the UAE 2031
  - Qatar National Vision 2030
  - Kuwait Vision 2035 (New Kuwait)
  - Bahrain Vision 2030
  - Oman Vision 2040
  - Egypt Vision 2030
- **Megaproject tracker**: Table of major projects with: name, country, estimated value, status (announced/under construction/completed), expected completion, latest news link
  - NEOM / The Line, Red Sea Global, Diriyah Gate, Qiddiya, Jeddah Tower
  - Masdar City, Saadiyat Island, Etihad Rail
  - Lusail City, FIFA legacy projects
  - Silk City (Kuwait), South Sabah Al-Ahmad
  - etc.
- **KPI dashboard**: Key metrics by country — non-oil GDP %, tourism arrivals, FDI inflows, privatization count — with latest available data and trend arrows

**Data source:**
- Curated and manually maintained initially (JSON/markdown files)
- Supplemented by news feed articles tagged "vision" or "megaproject"
- Updated monthly with latest data from government reports

## 3. Non-functional requirements

**Performance:**
- First Contentful Paint < 1.5s
- All pages should be server-rendered or ISR
- No client-side waterfall data fetching
- Compress all API responses with gzip

**Reliability:**
- All external API calls wrapped in try/catch with fallback to cached data
- If Yahoo Finance is down, show "data delayed" badge with last-known values
- If AI summarization fails, show the raw RSS description as fallback
- Vercel Cron failures should be logged but not break the UI

**Cost optimization:**
- Claude API: Batch summarize articles (10 at a time) to reduce API calls
- Yahoo Finance: Cache aggressively, max 1 call per symbol per 15 minutes
- Vercel: Stay within Hobby plan limits initially (100GB bandwidth, 100K function invocations)
- Postgres: Use efficient queries with proper indexes on country, sector, created_at

**Security:**
- No auth needed (personal use, deployed on private Vercel URL)
- All API keys in environment variables, never exposed client-side
- Rate limit the AI endpoint if exposed
